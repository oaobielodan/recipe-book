Korean Beef Power Bowls:

yield: 4 servings ~ prep: 30 mins ~ cook: 15 mins ~ total: 45 mins

ingredients

for beef:

  • 1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/4 cup reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sriracha, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound grounf beef

instructions

  1. in a small bowl, whisk together brown sugar, soy sauce, ginger, sesame oil, and sriracha
  2. heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium high head
  3. add garlic and cook, stirring constantly, unitl fragrant, about 1 minute
  4. add ground beef and cook until browned, about 3-5 minutes, making sure to cumble the beef as it cooks; drain excess fat
  5. stir in soy sauce mixture until well combined, allowing to simmer until heated through, about 2 minutes

Puff-Puff:

ingredients

  • 5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 small packets yeast
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • vegetable oil

instructions

  1. in a small bowl, mix all ingredients apart from the vegetable oil
  2. allow to sit until flour rises
  3. pour enough vegetbale oil to deep fry the dough mixture into a pot on medium heat
  4. fry mixture small, smalls balls (like size of a tennis ball)

Corn - Spoon Bread:

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 8 ozs of sour cream
  • 2 tablespoonful of sugar
  • 1 8.5 oz box of jiffy corn muffin mix or martha white corn muffin mix

instructions

  1. beat eggs lightly
  2. melt butter
  3. mix all ingredients together lightly by hand apart from the corn muffin mix
  4. lightly mix in box of corn muffin mix
  5. bake in a greased deep dish or cupcake pan for about 1 hour in a 300-350 degree oven unitl the tooth pick comes out clean
